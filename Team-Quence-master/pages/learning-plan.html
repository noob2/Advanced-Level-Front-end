Learning Plan

Curriculum

The curriculum is a combination of programming courses and technologies, entirely directed to the adoption of fundamental knowledge, developing logical and algorithmic thinking and working with the latest software technologies.

Forms of education

The forms of education in the Loonivercity are onsite and online. The lectures are held mostly in the evening, so that their visit is convenient for working students, who are in online form of education.

Diplomas and Certificates

Acquired certificates after studying at Loonivercity prove knowledge and skills you have mastered in certain technologies and can be presented to the companies-employers.

Scholarships

10% of the best students will be exempted from tuition for the upcoming level , receiving a scholarship for excellent success. Grantees of Loonivercity is determined individually at the start of each level.

Student Loan

Societe Generale Expressbank offers a special offer for a student loan to students of Loonivercity. The offer is valid for taken successful entrance exam in Loonivercity.

Open courses

Open courses can be attended not only by students of Loonivercity but also by external visitors. The courses cover different technological topics, improving the practical skills of the students.
